Story: Zog the Gentle Giant

In the year 2025, amidst the towering skyscrapers and the hum of advanced technology, Zog the caveman mysteriously appeared. Clad in his signature spotted fur and armed with incredible strength, Zog was an anomaly from the prehistoric era, but his kind heart and helpful nature quickly won people over.

Zog's strength was unmatched—he could lift cars with ease and even stop runaway buses. But what truly made him special was his kindness. He used his might not for destruction, but to assist those in need. Whether it was rescuing a kitten stuck in a tree or helping an old lady carry her groceries, Zog’s good deeds became the talk of the town.

One day, a massive earthquake hit the city, causing buildings to tremble and panic to spread. Zog stepped up, holding collapsing walls to protect families and clearing rubble to rescue trapped citizens. His bravery and selflessness saved countless lives.

Despite his caveman ways—like grunting in place of speaking—Zog taught the modern world an important lesson: strength means nothing without compassion. Soon, Zog became a beloved figure, a symbol of kindness in a world often too busy to notice the little things. The city even erected a statue in his honor, inscribed with the words: "Zog, the Gentle Giant—Proof that kindness is timeless."
